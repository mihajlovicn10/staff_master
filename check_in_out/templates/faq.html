Frequently Asked Questions

How to log in to an application?

To log in to this application, you need to become a member. To do it, go to register page and sign up.
In case you are already signed up, you just need to go to login page, and enter your username and your password. You can find a link to login page here.

How to sign up to the application?

If you are new to this platform, you need to sign up. To do this, go to the register page, fill in your personal data, username and password.
Afterwards, you become a member, and you can log in to a platform.

Application cannot work. What should I do?

If application cannot work on your device, here's what you are going to do. Check
First, make sure your internet connection is working properly.Check any cables and reboot any routers, modems, or other network devices you may be using.
Second, allow your browser to access the network in your firewall or antivirus settings. If it is already listed as a program allowed to access the network, try removing it from the list and adding it again.
If you are using proxy server, check your proxy settings or contact your network administrator to make sure the proxy server is working. If you don't believe you should be using a proxy server: go to the main menu > settings > change proxy settings > LAN settings and deselect "Use a proxy server for your LAN".

None of these options was successful. What should I do?

If none of these options cannot solve your problems, contact our tech support for further instructions.
